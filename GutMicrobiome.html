Gut Microbiome /biota

Microbiota and biome

  • Microbiome:It is the collective gene of a community of microorganism
  • Microbiota:Community of microorganism living in a specific environment

Function of Gut-microbiota

  • Absorption and digestion of nutrients
  • Immune system regulation
  • Protection against pathogen
  • Brain-gut communication: acteria communicate with brain through brain-gut axis
  • Metabolic regulation

Gut composition

  • Firmicutes: break down complex carbohydrate and produce short-chain fatty acids
  • Bacteroides: break down complex carbohydrate and polysaccharide
  • Actinobacteria: break down complex carbohydrate and produce vitamins

Treatment

  • Next-generation sequencing is used for detailed inspection of microbiota composition by using biomarkers gene sequencing
  • Metagenomics: provides information about the capacity of genes
  • Metatranscriptomatics: provides informations about activity of transciption
  • Maas spectometry: Analysis of produced products of bacteria
