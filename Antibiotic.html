Antibiotic

Alexander Flemming discovered the first antibiotic called Penicillin in 1928

How do antibiotic work?

    Antibiotics are:

  • Bacteriostatic:Prevent bacterial replication and give the host cell a chance to fight back the infection
  • Bactericidal: Kill bacteria

Antibiotic mechanism of action

  • Cell wall synthesis disruption
  • RNA / DNA synthesis disruption
  • Protein synthesis disruption

Antibiotic structure

  • Contain a beta-lactame ring
  • Disrupt cell wall synthesis, by binding to Penicillin-Binding Protein and prevent cross-linking of peptidogylcan

Biofilm

  • Quorum sensing is the communication of cell to cell of active high concentration of signal molecules and collective reaction

Mechanism of resistance

  • Degrading enzymes
  • Target alteration
  • Blockage of uptake
  • Efflux pump
