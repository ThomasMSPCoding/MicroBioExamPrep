Salmonella

Salmonella is a gram-negative bacteria from the familiy Enterobacteria. There are two species:Salmonella Bongori & Salmonella Enterica. This bacteria can survive weeks in difficult environement and months in water

Epidemiology

  • Colonize in animals
  • Serotype: Salmonella Typhi & Salmonella paratyphi:
  • adapted to humans and cause disease ->Typhoid fever

    • Pathogensis

    1)

  • Ingestion and passage through the stomach
  • Salmonella attach to the mucosa of the small intestine and invade the M cells located at the payers patch

    • 2)

  • Salmonella secreted proteins are injected into the host cells. The pathogenecity contains genes that invade the immune system of host cells

    • 3)

  • Inflammatory response confines the infection to the GI tract, mediates the release of prostaglandis and stimulates cAMP and fluid active secretion

    • Symptoms & Disease

  • Gastroenteritis
  • Enteric Fever
  • Septicemia
  • Asymptomatic carriage

    • Diagnosis & Treatment

  • Analysis based on signs and symptoms
  • Stool sample analysis
  • Treatment: fluid/medication intake and staying in bed
