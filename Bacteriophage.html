Bacteriophage

Mechanism of action

  • 1. Landing: The Bacteriophage attaches to the host cell surface
  • 2. Pinning: Bacteriophage pins himself to the host cell
  • 3. Penetration: Bacteriophage penetrates with its spike
  • 4. Injecting: Bacteriophage injects its DNA into the host cell
