ESBL: Extended Spectrum Beta Lactamase

It is a produced enzyme from Enterobacteria that inactivates the beta-lactame antibiotica


Ambler class of beta-lactamase

Class A: ESBL

Microorganism that produce ESBL enzyme are resistant to penicillin and 1-3rd generation of cephaloporins and monobactam. To overcome the resistance , beta-lactame must combine with beta-lactamase inhibitor

Class B: Metallo-beta-lactamase

Microorganism are resistant to various antibiotica

Class C: AmpC

Are resistant to all antibiotics except carbapenems

Class D: Oxacillinase

Are resistant to all antibiotics except carbapenems
